01 — About

Who I Am

I'm an AI practitioner and product thinker based in Nashville with a combination of backgrounds that's hard to find in one person: over a decade of senior business leadership in mortgage and finance, four years of backend software engineering on AWS and GCP, and hands-on experience building and shipping AI-powered tools in production.

That combination matters in AI adoption work. I can sit in a stakeholder meeting, understand exactly what business problem is being described, then walk into a technical conversation and translate it into something an engineering team can actually build. Most people live on one side of that line. I've deliberately built a career on both.

I'm targeting AI Business Analyst, Product Owner, and AI Program Manager roles at organizations serious about AI adoption. I'm not just talking about AI strategy — I've built agents, deployed APIs, and written the requirements to help teams do the same.
