Overview

What It Does

The AI BA Toolkit is a full-featured web application built to demonstrate real AI business analyst capabilities — not just the theory, but working tools that solve actual business problems. It was built in one week using React, Vite, and the Claude API, then deployed on Vercel with environment-variable-controlled access gating and billing limit detection.

The toolkit includes three purpose-built agents, each targeting a specific use case relevant to AI BA and product work: field sales validation, structured requirements generation, and small business research. It was built specifically to support job interviews and demonstrate the kind of work I can do — not just talk about.

Agents

Three Agents, Three Use Cases

// AGENT 01
Field Sales Business Validation Agent

One-click business lookup for field sales reps. Returns color-coded confidence scoring, source attribution distinguishing web data from CRM data, residential address flagging, and a PDF export of the full report. Includes proprietary data placeholders designed for CRM integration.

// AGENT 02
Requirements Analyst Agent

Takes vague stakeholder problem statements and transforms them into structured BA artifacts: user stories, acceptance criteria, edge cases, and an AI opportunity assessment mapped specifically to Microsoft Copilot and Azure AI Foundry. Designed to demonstrate BA methodology in action.

// AGENT 03
Small Business Research Agent

Uses live web search to surface business insights, competitive context, and opportunity signals relevant to small business owners and the organizations that advocate for them. Framed specifically for NFIB-style use cases.

// INFRASTRUCTURE
Access Gating and Error Handling

Environment-variable-controlled passcode gate keeps the demo controlled. Billing limit detection provides user-friendly error messaging when API limits are reached. Both were implemented to show production-readiness, not just a prototype.

Outcome

Results

Built and deployed in one week. Currently in active use as a live demo for hiring conversations. The toolkit is access-gated and shared selectively with specific hiring managers and interviewers. It directly demonstrates the core skillset being evaluated in AI BA roles: the ability to identify a real business need, design an AI solution around it, and ship something that works — not just describe how it would work.
